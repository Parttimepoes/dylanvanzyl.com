AWARD-SELECTED DOCUMENTARY

A Kid From Athlone

OFFICIAL SELECTION · SOUTHAMPTON FILM WEEK 2023

A short documentary following Jean-Marc Johannes — a skateboarder from Athlone, Cape Town. Raised in a tough environment, Jean-Marc found refuge on a board, turning hardship into discipline and, in time, a professional career.

Shot on home ground, the film traces his rise and the role he now plays for the next generation of skaters in his community — an Official Selection at Southampton Film Week 2023.

ABOUT

Stories told
through light.

I'm Dylan Van Zyl — a Cape Town–based cinematographer, director and photographer with seven years behind the lens. My work spans documentary, branded film, broadcast television and editorial portraiture.

I've shot as Director of Photography on a variety show that aired on Ginx Esports and was later acquired by Amazon Prime, directed the short film A Kid From Athlone — an Official Selection at Southampton Film Week 2023 — and currently lead corporate showcase production at DFY Studios.

Whatever the brief, my craft is the same: turning concepts into images that carry a brand's story and make people feel something.
